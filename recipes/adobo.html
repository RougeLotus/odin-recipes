Adobo

Description

A classic Filipino sweet and savory adobo recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken or Pork
  • 1 Onion
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 pineapple chunks 200g
  • Black pepper - cracked
  • Soy Sauce -
  • Vinegar -
  • Bay leaves
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Sear the meat until lightly browned.
  2. Add pineapple juice and vinegar. Wait until boiled to add the soy sauce and water.
  3. Lower the heat and simmer until the meat is cooked then add the pineapple chunks and bay leaves.
  4. Continue to cook until sauce is reduced to your liking.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
Recipe from Kawaling Pinoy
