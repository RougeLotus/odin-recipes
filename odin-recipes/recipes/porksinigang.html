Pork Sinigang

Description

A simple Filipino sour pork stew.

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Sinigang sa Sampalok Mix
  • Onion
  • Black pepper and salt.
  • Okra
  • String Beans
  • Eggplants

Steps

  1. Sautee the onion and the meat until browned.
  2. Add water and sinigang mix then boil until the meat is tender.
  3. Add the vegetables then simmer until vegetables are cooked.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
