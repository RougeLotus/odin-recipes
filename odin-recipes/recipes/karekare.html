Kare Kare

Description

A hearty oxtail stew with peanut sauce.

Ingredients

  • Oxtail
  • Peanut butter
  • Eggplants
  • String Beans
  • Water
  • Salt and pepper
  • Pechay
  • Shrimp Paste

Steps

  1. Boil water.
  2. Put the oxtail and onions and simmer until tender.
  3. Once the meat is tender, add peanut butter and simmer for 5 to 7 minutes.
  4. Put the eggplants and string beans in and cook for 5 minutes.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Serve with shrimp paste.
